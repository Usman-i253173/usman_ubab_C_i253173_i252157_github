About us

Welcome to PLAYSPOT, your new headquarters for play. Our mission is simple: to get more people active and make access to sports facilities seamless for everyone. We saw that finding and booking a court was often the biggest hurdle to getting a game in.

That's why we created a single, streamlined platform to browse, book, and pay for your next tennis match, basketball run, or badminton game in just a few clicks. Stop searching and start playing.

Values

1. Accessibility

We make sports easy to reach by giving everyone simple, fast access to nearby courts and facilities.

2. Convenience
